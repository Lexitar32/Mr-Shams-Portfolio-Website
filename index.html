About me

Shamsudeen Adedapo Aderoju is a vibrant Nigerian Youth with a thorough background in sciences and vast experience in leadership and community development. He is an alumnus of the prestigious British Council Leaders for Tomorrow Training program and a fellow of the Young African Leaders Initiative (West African Regional Leadership Centre). He currently works with TIIDELab Initiative as the Program Coordinator.

Shamsudeen has a seven-year experience facilitating workshops and seminars on leadership, community development, Nation and Community Development related topics. He is a seasoned facilitator, speaker, anchor and program executor. His life ambition is to raise future leaders from amongst the downtrodden. He is an excellent communicator, a writer, a speaker and a team player. He has a masters and Bachelor Degrees from the University of Ilorin and the Usmanu Danfodiyo University Sokoto Respectively. He is an advocate of peace and a staunch believer of the work-ability of the Nigerian project.
